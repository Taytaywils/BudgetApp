My Story

Budgeting is daunting, I know. I was at the beginning of it once, but as you heard in the video, it has literally changed my life. I used to be so stressed about money and finances. My wife and I would talk all the time about our goals but we didn't know how we were going to accomplish them. That is when we decided to take control of our finances.

My parents raised us talking about finances and how to make smart financial decisions, how to save, the importance and power of investing, and more. It was obvious from a young age that this was important to them and that they wanted it to be important for their kids as well.
